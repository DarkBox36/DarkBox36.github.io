Puzzle House

by DeepDish36

About the Game

A minimalist puzzle experience set inside a strange, silent house. Each room presents a unique challenge that tests your logic, perception, and curiosity.

You stumble upon an unfamiliar home with no instructions and no clear purpose. The only way forward is through the puzzles—ten rooms, ten different rules, and no hand‑holding.

Every level teaches you something new. Some rely on sound, others on light, memory, or observation. Nothing is random. Everything has a solution.

Features

Atmospheric Exploration

Navigate through a mysterious house where each room has its own mood, lighting, and hidden logic.

Environmental Mystery

Discover the house's secrets through subtle environmental clues and the behavior of each puzzle.

Puzzle Variety

Solve ten unique puzzles ranging from sound-based challenges to logic, memory, and perception tests.

Progressive Challenge

Every level introduces a new mechanic, keeping the experience fresh, surprising, and progressively more challenging.
